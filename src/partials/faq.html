Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers. If you can't find what you're looking for, feel free to contact our support team.

What is BrieflyAI? ▼

BrieflyAI is an AI-powered copilot designed to help you quickly summarize text, generate creative content, and answer questions from your documents. It aims to boost your productivity and streamline your workflow.

How accurate are the summaries? ▼

Our AI models are highly advanced and trained on vast datasets, ensuring a high level of accuracy for summaries. We focus on extracting the most critical information while preserving the original context.

What file types can I upload for Q&A? ▼

BrieflyAI supports various file types for document Q&A, including PDF, DOCX, TXT, and common image formats (for OCR). We are continuously expanding our supported formats.

Is my data private and secure? ▼

Yes, data security and privacy are paramount. We use industry-standard encryption and strict privacy protocols to ensure your information is always protected. We do not store your content for training purposes unless explicitly opted-in.

Do you offer a free trial? ▼

Yes, we offer a free tier that allows you to experience the core features of BrieflyAI. You can upgrade to a paid plan at any time for more extensive usage and advanced features.
